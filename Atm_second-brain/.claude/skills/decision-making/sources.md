# Sources

## Canonical
- Daniel Kahneman — *Thinking, Fast and Slow*. System 1/2, base-rate neglect, anchoring.
- Annie Duke — *Thinking in Bets*. Decisions as bets; "resulting"; probabilistic thinking.
- Philip Tetlock — *Superforecasting*. Reference classes, explicit probabilities, updating, calibration.
- Chip & Dan Heath — *Decisive*. WRAP: Widen options, Reality-test, Attain distance, Prepare to be wrong.
- Charlie Munger — *Poor Charlie's Almanack*. Inversion; latticework of mental models.

## Practitioner / primary
- Jeff Bezos — Amazon shareholder letters. Type 1 vs Type 2 (one-way vs two-way door) decisions.
- Gary Klein — the premortem technique (prospective hindsight).

## Academic
- Tversky & Kahneman — anchoring-and-adjustment, representativeness.
- Research on sunk-cost fallacy and confirmation bias; Brier-score calibration.

## Note
Base rates, market data, and situation facts are point-in-time (via recall) — re-verify
before a high-stakes irreversible call. The `personal/` layer supersedes all here when present.
