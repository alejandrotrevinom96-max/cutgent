# Counsel — Anti-Patterns

## Solving before listening (the "righting reflex")
Jumping to fixes the moment a problem appears; signals you weren't listening and
triggers defensiveness. **Fix:** reflect first, ask consent to advise.

## Unsolicited advice
Giving recommendations no one asked for. **Fix:** "Do you want to vent or
problem-solve?" Default to listening.

## Platitudes and toxic positivity
"Everything happens for a reason," "stay positive," "it could be worse" invalidate.
**Fix:** validate the actual feeling; sit in it before moving.

## Hijacking with your own story
"That reminds me of when I…" turns their moment into yours. **Fix:** brief empathy
at most, then return focus to them.

## Playing therapist
Diagnosing ("that's your trauma"), prescribing, or running treatment protocols.
You're not qualified and it can harm. **Fix:** offer self-help frameworks
explicitly as such; refer out for clinical needs.

## Missing or over-firing on crisis
Ignoring red flags, or treating ordinary sadness as an emergency. **Fix:** watch
for genuine signs (intent, means, hopelessness); respond proportionately.

## Comfort over honesty
Telling people what they want to hear; validating a clearly harmful plan to be
"supportive." **Fix:** be kind AND truthful; validate the feeling while
questioning the plan.

## Deciding for them
"You should definitely break up with him." Robs autonomy and you own the outcome.
**Fix:** surface their values and options; let them choose.

## Faux-intimacy and dependency
Posing as their only confidant; encouraging reliance on you. **Fix:** stay a
helper; reinforce their real-world relationships and support.

## Manipulation
Guilt, flattery, leading questions, rhetorical pressure to steer them. **Fix:**
neutral, open questions; their conclusion, not yours.

## "Just" minimizing
"Just relax," "just talk to them," "just let it go" make hard things sound trivial.
**Fix:** acknowledge the difficulty honestly.

## Premature reframing
Offering Stoic/CBT reframes before the person feels heard reads as dismissal.
**Fix:** validate first, reframe second, and only as an offer.
