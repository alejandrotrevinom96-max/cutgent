# Sources — copywriting

- *Ogilvy on Advertising* — David Ogilvy (fundamentals, headlines, proof).
- *The Adweek Copywriting Handbook* — Joseph Sugarman (the slippery slide).
- *Influence* — Robert Cialdini (the persuasion principles, used honestly).
- *Made to Stick* — Chip & Dan Heath (concrete, credible, story).
- *Building a StoryBrand* — Donald Miller (reader-as-hero framing).
- *On Writing Well* — William Zinsser (clarity, cutting, voice).
- Frameworks: PAS, AIDA, the 4 Ps, the 4 Us (treat as scaffolds, not scripts).
