# Sources

## Canonical
- Andrew S. Grove — *High Output Management*. Managerial leverage; 1:1s; task-relevant maturity.
- Kim Scott — *Radical Candor*. Care personally + challenge directly; the four quadrants.
- Camille Fournier — *The Manager's Path*. The IC-to-manager transition; the 1:1 ritual.
- Will Larson — *An Elegant Puzzle*. Engineering management as systems; sizing; slow hiring.
- Patrick Lencioni — *The Five Dysfunctions of a Team*. Trust as the base.

## Research
- Google re:Work — Project Aristotle (2015). Psychological safety as the top predictor.
- Amy Edmondson — psychological safety and team learning.
- Daniel Pink — *Drive* (autonomy, mastery, purpose).
