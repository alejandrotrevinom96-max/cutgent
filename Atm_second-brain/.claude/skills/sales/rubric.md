# Sales Rubric (BINARY — every box passes before advancing or forecasting)

- [ ] The account was researched and confirmed to fit the ICP before outreach.
- [ ] Discovery was done before any pitch or proposal (diagnose before prescribe).
- [ ] The buyer's actual problem is documented in their own words.
- [ ] The impact/cost of that problem is quantified (current vs desired state — the gap).
- [ ] The cost of inaction ("do nothing") is made explicit in the buyer's numbers.
- [ ] The buyer did the majority of the talking during discovery.
- [ ] The economic buyer (controls budget, can say yes) is identified and reachable.
- [ ] The decision criteria and process/timeline to signature are known.
- [ ] A champion (internal advocate with influence) is identified.
- [ ] Value is tied to the buyer's own success metric, not to features.
- [ ] Objections were understood (asked about) before being addressed — not steamrolled.
- [ ] The deal is multithreaded: more than one stakeholder relationship exists.
- [ ] Every call ends with a specific, scheduled next step (date, owner, purpose).
- [ ] The close is explicitly asked for once value and qualification are established.
- [ ] Honest-fit check passed: the product genuinely solves their problem, or the deal is walked away from.
- [ ] No manipulation, manufactured urgency, or pressure tactics are used anywhere.
- [ ] `personal/` recall has been consulted; any conflict with generic advice is flagged and personal/ wins.
