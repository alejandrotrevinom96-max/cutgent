# Sales — Sources

- **Neil Rackham, *SPIN Selling* (1988)** — research-based question-led discovery.
- **Dixon & Adamson, *The Challenger Sale* (2011)** — Teach-Tailor-Take control.
- **Keenan, *Gap Selling* (2018)** — current vs future state; the gap is the value.
- **Mike Weinberg, *New Sales. Simplified.* (2012)** — target list, sales story, outbound.
- **MEDDIC / MEDDPICC** (PTC, 1990s) — Metrics, Economic buyer, Decision criteria,
  Decision process, Identify pain, Champion (+ Paper, Competition).
- **BANT** (IBM) — Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline.

## Notes
Method here is durable; tooling specifics (CRM features, outreach-automation rules,
deliverability/spam law) change — verify those against current sources. Selling
establishes a qualified, willing buyer; price/terms bargaining belongs to the
`negotiation` pack and comes after the sale is won in principle.
