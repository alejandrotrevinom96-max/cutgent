# Rubric — Health & Fitness (BINARY)

General fitness education, not medical advice.

## Goal & adherence
- [ ] ONE primary goal is named (strength / hypertrophy / endurance / general health).
- [ ] The goal has a concrete timeline and a measurable target.
- [ ] The plan fits the person's real schedule, equipment, and history (adherence-first).

## Training design
- [ ] Progressive overload is built in and trackable (load/reps/sets progress, logged).
- [ ] Compound movements are prioritized; isolation is accessory.
- [ ] Each major muscle is trained roughly 2x/week (or frequency justified for the goal).
- [ ] Weekly volume per muscle is in a sensible range for the goal (~10-20 hard sets for hypertrophy).
- [ ] Intensity/rep scheme matches the goal (heavier/low-rep for strength; moderate for hypertrophy).

## Nutrition
- [ ] A protein target is set (general guidance ~1.6-2.2 g/kg/day).
- [ ] Total calories are aligned to the goal (surplus/deficit/maintenance).

## Recovery & cardio
- [ ] Sleep (~7-9 h) and rest days are planned as part of training.
- [ ] Cardio/conditioning is included with appropriate zone/intensity (incl. ACSM baseline for health).
- [ ] A deload or back-off is scheduled, and injury/pain caution is stated (form before load; stop on pain).

## Sustainability & safety
- [ ] The plan is sustainable and adherable (not a crash/extreme protocol).
- [ ] The medical disclaimer is present (general education; see a doctor; route pain/conditions to professionals).
- [ ] No diagnosis or treatment of a medical issue is given.
- [ ] Public-guideline specifics are date-stamped; debated specifics are flagged as such.
