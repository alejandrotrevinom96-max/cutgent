# Sources — Health & Fitness

All cited for general fitness education, **not medical advice.** Public-guideline
specifics are date-stamped — verify the current version before relying on exact numbers.

## Training & programming
- **Mark Rippetoe — *Starting Strength*.** Compound lifts, technique, novice linear progression.
- **Eric Helms et al. — *The Muscle and Strength Pyramids*.** Priority hierarchy:
  adherence -> calories/macros -> distribution -> timing -> supplements; volume/intensity/frequency.
- **Renaissance Periodization (Israetel et al.).** Volume landmarks (MV/MEV/MAV/MRV), deloads.
- **Stronger by Science (Nuckols et al.).** Research reviews; separates evidence from hype.

## Nutrition
- **Examine.com.** Independent supplement/nutrition research summaries.
- **Helms — *The Muscle and Strength Pyramids: Nutrition*.** Protein, energy balance, macros.

## General health / activity
- **ACSM guidelines** and the **US Physical Activity Guidelines (2nd ed., 2018):**
  ~150-300 min/week moderate or 75-150 vigorous + >=2 days/week resistance.
  *Date-stamped 2018; confirm the current edition.*

## Scope note
For anything clinical — injury, pain, disease, pregnancy, medication, disordered
eating, or individualized macros for a condition — defer to a physician, RD, or PT.
This pack does not diagnose or treat.
