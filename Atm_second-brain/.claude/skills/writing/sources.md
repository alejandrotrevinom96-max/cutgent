# Sources — Writing

- **William Zinsser — *On Writing Well*.** Clarity, simplicity, brevity, humanity; editing as removing clutter.
- **Strunk & White — *The Elements of Style*.** "Omit needless words"; concision/active-voice discipline.
  **Critique:** overreaches and is sometimes wrong (esp. on passive voice — see Pullum,
  "50 Years of Stupid Grammar Advice," 2009). Instinct, not dogma; not a final authority.
- **Verlyn Klinkenborg — *Several Short Sentences About Writing*.** The sentence as the unit; varied, well-made sentences.
- **Anne Lamott — *Bird by Bird*.** The "shitty first draft"; separating generating from editing.
- **Steven Pinker — *The Sense of Style*.** Classic style; the curse of knowledge; evidence-based usage.
- **John McPhee — *Draft No. 4*.** Structure/spine; revision as the real work.

## Scope note
This pack is for long-form nonfiction prose. For conversion-driven marketing copy
(headlines, landing pages, CTAs) use the **copywriting** pack.
