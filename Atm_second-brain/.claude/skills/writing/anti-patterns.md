# Anti-patterns — Writing

## Idea & structure
- **No controlling idea.** Can't say what it's about in one sentence. *Fix:* pick one.
- **Prose before structure.** Lovely paragraphs that don't add up. *Fix:* outline first, rebuild.
- **Burying the lede / endless windup.** *Fix:* move the subject up; cut the runway.

## Drafting & revision
- **Shipping the first draft.** *Fix:* run structure -> cut -> sentence -> read-aloud passes.
- **Editing while drafting (paralysis).** *Fix:* allow a bad draft 1; separate generate from edit.

## Sentence-level
- **Abstraction haze.** All concepts, no examples. *Fix:* add specifics.
- **Weak verbs / nominalizations / passive overuse.** *Fix:* strong active verb.
- **Monotone rhythm.** Every sentence the same length. *Fix:* vary length; let a short one land.
- **Adverb/qualifier padding.** "very," "I think that." *Fix:* delete; choose a precise word.
- **Telling not showing.** *Fix:* dramatize/evidence; let the reader conclude.

## Voice & honesty
- **Inflated jargon "smart voice."** *Fix:* plain words, classic style.
- **Padding to length.** *Fix:* cut; quality over quantity.
- **Fabrication.** Invented facts/quotes/stats. *Fix:* remove or source honestly. Never invent.
- **Strunk-&-White-as-scripture.** Mechanically banning all passives. *Fix:* apply judgment.
- **Generic voice.** Ignoring the user's known style. *Fix:* recall the personal layer and match it.
