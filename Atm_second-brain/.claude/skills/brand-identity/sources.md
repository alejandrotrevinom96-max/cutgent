# Sources

## Canonical (core method)
- Marty Neumeier — *The Brand Gap* & *Zag*. Brand = a gut feeling, not a logo;
  differentiate or die; the strategy-to-execution bridge; "onlyness" statement.
- Alina Wheeler — *Designing Brand Identity*. End-to-end identity-system process:
  research, clarify strategy, design identity, create touchpoints, manage assets.
- Jenni Romaniuk & the Ehrenberg-Bass Institute — *Building Distinctive Brand
  Assets* / *How Brands Grow* (with Byron Sharp). Distinctive assets, fame +
  uniqueness, mental availability, consistency over reinvention.
- Al Ries & Jack Trout — *Positioning: The Battle for Your Mind*. Owning a position
  in the audience's mind; the strategy that visuals must serve.
- Debbie Millman — *Brand Thinking* (and *Design Matters*). Why brands mean what
  they mean; meaning and narrative behind identity.
- Wally Olins — *The Brand Handbook* / *On Brand*. Corporate identity systems and
  coherence across a whole organization's touchpoints.

## Verbal identity, type & color
- Mailchimp *Content Style Guide* (voiceandtone.com lineage) — voice vs tone, tone
  mapped to emotional context; a working model for verbal identity.
- Nielsen Norman Group — *The Four Dimensions of Tone of Voice*.
- Ellen Lupton — *Thinking with Type*. Type systems, pairing, hierarchy.
- Josef Albers — *Interaction of Color*; WCAG 2.x contrast guidance (AA/AAA) for
  accessible color systems.

## Studios & cases to study (mechanism, not imitation)
- Pentagram, Collins, DesignStudio, Wolff Olins, Chermayeff & Geismar — published
  case studies; read the *rationale*, not just the reels.

## Note on freshness
Design trends, font licensing terms, platform asset specs (favicon/app-icon sizes,
social avatar crops, dark-mode requirements), and competitor identities are
point-in-time — re-verify before shipping. Trend currency is the weakest reason to
choose anything; strategy and distinctiveness outlast it. The `personal/` layer
(the user's own brand strategy, audience, existing assets, voice, and prior
decisions) supersedes everything here when present.
