# Negotiation Anti-Patterns (detectable failure modes + fix)

## 1. No BATNA / unknown walk-away
**Tell:** can't name what you'll do if the deal dies; you "need" this deal.
**Fix:** define and, if weak, improve your BATNA before talking. Set a concrete
reservation point. You cannot hold firm on a floor you never set.

## 2. Negotiating against yourself
**Tell:** you make a second concession before they respond to your first; you
soften your own offer to fill a silence.
**Fix:** state your number, then stop. Let them respond. One move per turn.

## 3. Splitting the difference reflexively
**Tell:** "let's just meet in the middle" used as a default to avoid discomfort.
**Fix:** the midpoint of two anchors is arbitrary, and it rewards extreme openers.
Re-anchor on rationale/data, or trade across issues instead.

## 4. Weak or unjustified anchor
**Tell:** opening at a round, defensive, easily-dismissed number with no reason.
**Fix:** anchor at the edge of the *defensible* range and attach a justification.
A reasoned aggressive anchor pulls the outcome; a timid one cedes it.

## 5. Arguing positions, ignoring interests
**Tell:** the conversation is a tug-of-war over one variable (usually price).
**Fix:** ask why each side wants what it wants; find an unshared interest and an
issue you value differently, then trade.

## 6. Unilateral concessions (no trade-for)
**Tell:** "Okay, I can do that" given freely to seem agreeable.
**Fix:** every give is conditional: "If you can X, then I can Y." Concede in
decreasing increments to signal the floor.

## 7. Emotional leakage / reactive devaluation
**Tell:** visible frustration, ultimatums in anger, or rejecting an offer because
*they* proposed it.
**Fix:** label your own and their emotions, slow down, evaluate the offer on
merits vs your reservation point — not on who said it.

## 8. No authority / deadline check
**Tell:** you negotiate hard, then "I have to run it by my boss/legal" surfaces.
**Fix:** confirm both sides' authority to commit and the real deadline up front;
otherwise you anchor against a phantom and concede against a fake clock.
