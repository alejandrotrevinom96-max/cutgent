# Communication — Sources

- Chris Anderson — *TED Talks: The Official TED Guide to Public Speaking* (2016).
  One idea worth spreading; throughline; hooks; delivery.
- Chip & Dan Heath — *Made to Stick* (2007). SUCCES; the curse of knowledge.
- Pixar story spine (Emma Coats / Pixar storytelling principles).
- Nancy Duarte — *slide:ology* (2008); *Resonate* (2010). One idea per slide;
  audience as hero.
- Garr Reynolds — *Presentation Zen* (2008). Simplicity; slides as billboards.
- Barbara Minto — *The Pyramid Principle* (1987). Answer first; MECE; BLUF.
- Stone, Patton & Heen — *Difficult Conversations*. Patterson et al. — *Crucial
  Conversations*. Shared purpose, facts before story, make it safe.

Adapt to the specific audience, culture, and medium; the personal layer overrides.
