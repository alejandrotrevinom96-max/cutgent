# Communication

Make the message land. The default failure is dumping everything you know; the
craft is choosing one thing and delivering it so the audience remembers and acts.

## Method (in order)

1. **Audience and goal first.** Who's in the room? What do they believe, want,
   fear? What do you want them to *think, feel, or do* afterward? Everything serves
   that.
2. **One core message.** State it in a single sentence. If you can't, you have more
   than one talk. (TED: one idea worth spreading.)
3. **Structure the journey.** Hook → tension → resolution. Open by earning
   attention; create a gap or stakes; resolve it. Use the Pixar spine or Minto's
   top-down pyramid (answer first, then support) per context.
4. **Clarity over cleverness.** Plain words, concrete examples, no unshared jargon.
   If they have to decode it, you've lost them.
5. **Make it stick.** Concrete, credible, unexpected, emotional, story-driven
   (Heath's SUCCES). One vivid example beats five abstractions.
6. **Signpost.** Tell them the shape ("three things"), mark transitions, recap.
   Audiences can't rewind; navigation is your job.
7. **Slides are minimal.** One idea per slide; images and few words over bullet
   walls. Slides support you; they aren't your notes.
8. **Delivery is craft.** Rehearse aloud, on your feet, timed. Vary pace; use the
   pause as punctuation; project and breathe; eye contact; cut filler.
9. **Plan Q&A.** Anticipate the hard questions, prepare honest answers, bridge back
   to the core message. "I don't know — I'll follow up" beats bluffing.

## Difficult conversations

Lead with the shared goal, state observations not accusations, make your point once
and clearly, then *listen*. Separate the person from the problem; make the ask
specific. (See `exemplars.md`.)

## Personal layer overrides

The user's `personal/` context (their speaking style, audiences, what's worked,
role, prior talks, natural voice), via recall, **overrides this generic method**
where they conflict. Generic craft is the default; their reality wins.
