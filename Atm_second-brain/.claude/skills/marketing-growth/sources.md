# Marketing & Growth — Sources

- **April Dunford, *Obviously Awesome* (2019)** — the five-component positioning process.
- **Sean Ellis & Morgan Brown, *Hacking Growth* (2017)** — north-star, activation, high-tempo testing.
- **Dave McClure — "Startup Metrics for Pirates" (AARRR)** — the funnel framing.
- **Weinberg & Mares, *Traction* (2015)** — 19 channels + Bullseye.
- **Brian Balfour — "Four Fits" / Reforge essays** — market/product/channel/model fit.
- **Donald Miller, *Building a StoryBrand* (2017)** — customer-as-hero, the one-liner.

## Freshness note (IMPORTANT)
Books above are durable on *method*. Anything platform-, algorithm-, or ad-spec-
specific (ranking signals, ad formats/policies, attribution, CPMs/benchmarks)
changes constantly — date-stamp it, verify against the live platform, and treat
older claims as suspect. Prefer a fresh search over memory for current platform behavior.
