# Marketing & Growth — Anti-patterns

- **Tactics before positioning.** "Let's run ads" with no who/alternative/value.
  *Detect:* no one-sentence positioning. *Fix:* do positioning first.
- **"Everyone is our customer."** ICP is a whole industry. *Fix:* name the exact
  person, their alternative, where they are.
- **Spreading thin.** Small budgets on six channels, no signal. *Fix:* bullseye —
  test cheap, commit to the winner.
- **Channel/model mismatch.** Low-ACV product funding high-touch sales. *Fix:* does
  CAC pay back at this price? Do the arithmetic.
- **Acquisition into a leaky bucket.** Scaling spend while retention is poor. *Fix:*
  fix activation/retention first.
- **Vanity metrics.** Signups, impressions, followers. *Fix:* use the north-star
  (proves value/revenue).
- **Multi-variable experiments.** Changing headline + price + audience at once.
  *Fix:* one variable, one metric, pre-set rule.
- **Overclaiming / fake scarcity.** "#1", fake countdowns. *Fix:* only substantiated
  claims; real scarcity only.
- **Brand-as-hero messaging.** Copy about how passionate the company is. *Fix:*
  customer is the hero, brand the guide.
- **Undated platform tactics.** "The algorithm rewards X" with no date. *Fix:* stamp
  "as of <date>" and re-check; platform specifics decay.
