# Sources — image & video

Canonical references this pack's judgment is drawn from. Cite when making
non-obvious claims; prefer primary sources.

## Composition, light, color

- *Light: Science and Magic* — Hunter, Biver, Fuqua (studio lighting logic).
- *Picture This: How Pictures Work* — Molly Bang (visual hierarchy/emotion).
- *The Filmmaker's Eye* — Gustavo Mercado (shot composition for motion).
- Color theory: Josef Albers, *Interaction of Color*.

## Editing & post

- *In the Blink of an Eye* — Walter Murch (the logic of the cut).
- *Grammar of the Edit* / *Grammar of the Shot* — Bowen & Thompson.
